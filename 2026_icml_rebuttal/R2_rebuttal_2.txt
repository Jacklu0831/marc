## (W1 + Q1) Stronger Models for ARC.

We appreciate the suggestion and scale our ARC evaluation to Qwen3-14B and Qwen3-32B, both fully off-the-shelf with **no task-specific fine-tuning**. These are among the strongest open-source dense models available. For reference, GPT-4o achieves approximately 5% on ARC-AGI-1 with direct few-shot prompting [1], establishing that low absolute accuracy on ARC is characteristic of all LLMs under direct generation (without Chain-of-Thought or program synthesis), not a limitation of any particular method.

|Model|ICL|CT-KV (LOO)|
|-|-|-|
|Qwen3-8B|3.5%|4.5%|
|Qwen3-14B|5.1%|5.6%|
|Qwen3-32B|6.6%|**7.1%**|

CT-KV consistently improves over ICL at every scale. Notably, Qwen3-32B's ICL accuracy (6.6%) already exceeds GPT-4o's published ~5%, and CT-KV pushes it further to 7.1%. Combined with our existing results scaling from 1B to 32B on BBH and MMLU (Section 5.6, Table 2), CT-KV demonstrates consistent gains across model scales and benchmarks.
